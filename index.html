Portal dedicated to train delays in Europe

In the European Union, 9.8% of trains arrived late. In 2021 railways took 5,200,000 passengers to 36,000 stations. The number of minutes that passengers lost through train delays is uncountable. The travelDelay group will allow you to plan your journey down to one extra minute.
